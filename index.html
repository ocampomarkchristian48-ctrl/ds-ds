Mind Logic

Instructions

Welcome to Mind Logic! This web game has 3 modes: Easy Mode, Normal Mode, and Hard Mode. In this game, you will answer questions related to Discrete Structures lessons such as Sets, Relations, Functions, Logic, and Probability. Each mode contains shuffled questions. You must reach at least 16 points to win the game. If your score is below 16 points, the game will restart from the beginning and all questions will be shuffled again. Good Luck!
